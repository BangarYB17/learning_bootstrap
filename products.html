Vivo

Vivo is a global smartphone manufacturer known for offering a wide range of Android phones — from flagship camera-focused devices and performance-oriented series (like X and V) to affordable 5G models in the Y and T series. Their phones often feature large batteries, fast charging, high-refresh-rate displays, and camera systems co-engineered with optics partners like ZEISS.

Oppo

OPPO is a well-known smartphone manufacturer offering phones across budget, mid-range, and premium categories. Their devices often focus on camera capabilities, fast charging, sleek design, and ColorOS software features. India is one of OPPO’s key markets, with frequent model launches tailored to local preferences

Samsung

Many Samsung phones now support 6 years of Android and security updates — a strong longevity feature. Samsung’s One UI offers Galaxy AI enhancements like object removal, image editing tools, and productivity boosts.

Apple

Apple Inc. is a leading US tech company best known for its premium iPhones, iPads, Macs, Apple Watches, AirPods, and more. Its products are tightly integrated across software (iOS, macOS, watchOS) and services (Apple Music, iCloud, Apple Pay, Apple Intelligence), offering a smooth user experience across devices.
